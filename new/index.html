About us

We make apps centered around 4 main focuses

Minimal

From our apps to our wallpapers, everything we create is designed with a strong emphasis on minimalism.

Open-Source

We are dedicated to contributing to the Open-Source community because after all, software is meant to be free and open.

Right Brainy

What sets our apps apart from the rest is an obsession over user interface and experience design. A functional app is great, but without an appealing interface who would want to use it?

Intuitive

There is a reason why our logo is a pen. It's because it is arguably one of the most intuitive tools ever. We aim to make our software like a pen: stupidly easy to use.
