About

Protos is greek for first, and we aim to develop apps that are the first of their kind. Our apps are based on 4 main focuses

Minimal

From our apps to our wallpapers, everything we create is designed with a strong emphasis on minimalism.

Open-Source

We are dedicated to contributing to the Open-Source community because after all, software is meant to be free and open.

Right Brainey

What sets our software apart from the competition is an obsession over user interface and experience design. A functional app is great, but without an appealing interface who would want to use it?

Intuitive

There is a reason our logo is a pen. It's because it is arguably one of the most intuitive tools ever. We aim to make our software like a pen: stupidly easy to use.
